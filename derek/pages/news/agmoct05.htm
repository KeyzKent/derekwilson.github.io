Single Rooms

How to deal with requests for single rooms for the single people was discussed and the following ruling was put forward for voting on by all members

  • If you want to have your own room and are prepared to pay a premium for this you need to indicate this at the planning stage of the trip
  • We then need to establish the minimum number of rooms needed (based on not necessarily getting your single room request) and maximum number of rooms needed (to cater for all single room requests).
  • Depending on nature of trip planned and availability of number of rooms required to cater for the single rooms requested it may be necessary to make a judgement call if we cannot get enough rooms. Single people will then need to be approached to see if they mind sharing.
  • A suggested single room supplement has been calculated as follows:
  • Cost of Accommodation divided by number of rooms
  • Cost of Accommodation divided by BOBS members attending
  • Charge 50% of the difference between the two
  • When considering non BOBS members for a trip we will have to look at each case and the accommodation
  • When considering non BOBS members for a trip we will have to look at each case and the accommodation available at the time to see whether or not we can open the trip to them
