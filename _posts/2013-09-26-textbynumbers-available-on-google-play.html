This year I’ve been working on my first real project using HTML5 and I think its gone pretty well. I’ve even used PhoneGap to use the assets in an Android application.

I thought I would take the final step and publish the app on Google Play. I have to say its a pretty painless process (though its not free) and its well documented on the Google developers site as well as this post.

The application is now available, and the source code is public as well.

It was written to solve a particular problem an particular person was having, I guess I’d like to revisit it in future and make it more generally useful by adding in application editing of snippets.
